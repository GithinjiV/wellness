Meet Your Provider

Founder & Lead Psychiatrist

Dr. Sarah Chen, MD

With over 15 years of experience in psychiatry, I founded Evenna Mental Wellness with a simple belief: healing happens in the context of a trusting relationship.

My approach combines evidence-based psychiatric treatment with genuine human connection. I believe that when you feel truly heard and understood, real transformation becomes possible.

I completed my psychiatric residency at Johns Hopkins and have specialized training in trauma-informed care, medication management, and integrative psychiatry. But beyond credentials, what drives my practice is a deep commitment to meeting each person exactly where they are.

The principles that guide us.

Six core beliefs shape every interaction, every treatment plan, and every moment of care we provide.

01

Your Time, Your Pace

Healing isn't linear—it's deeply personal. We honor your unique journey, meeting you exactly where you are. There's no rushing, no pressure, just steady support as you find your way forward.

02

Safety First

Your vulnerability is sacred. We've created a judgment-free sanctuary where you can speak freely, knowing your privacy is fiercely protected and your trust deeply honored.

03

Compassion Always

You're never just a diagnosis or a case file. You're a whole person with a story worth hearing. Genuine empathy guides every conversation, every decision, every moment we share.

"

We believe in the quiet power of being truly seen.

04

Whole-Person Care

Symptoms tell only part of the story. We see beyond them—to your history, your hopes, your circumstances. Understanding the complete picture allows us to provide care that truly fits your life.

05

Evidence-Based

Our methods aren't guesswork—they're grounded in the latest research and proven approaches. You deserve treatments that work, backed by science and refined through experience.

06

Accessibility

Quality mental health care shouldn't be a privilege—it's a right. Through telehealth, we bring expert care directly to you, wherever you are, whenever you need it.

These aren't just words on a page—they're promises we keep, every single day.
